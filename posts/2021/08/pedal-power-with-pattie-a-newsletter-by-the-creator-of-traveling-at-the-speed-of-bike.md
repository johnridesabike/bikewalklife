Pattie Baker is starting a newsletter series in September called Pedal Power with Pattie, which are designed to align with her bike classes.

[You can subscribe here](https://pedalpower.substack.com/p/coming-soon).

> Welcome to Pedal Power with Pattie — a series of seven fun and helpful free weekly newsletters September 2 — October 7, 2021. They will align with my proprietary [Pedal Power with Pattie classes](https://travelingatthespeedofbike.com/empowering-women-and-girls/), designed to encourage you to ride a bike while centering your power and joy!

On her blog, Traveling at the Speed of Bike, [she has a post highlighting her "You Go, Girl!" free resources](https://travelingatthespeedofbike.com/2021/08/15/you-go-girl-complete-package-of-freebies/). These include Metro Atlanta bike routes, profiles of people advocating for inclusive bicycling, the aforementioned newsletter, and more.

The annual People for Bikes Community Survey is here until October 15th.
Completing it helps them rank the bikeability of cities across the US.

> Take the [**PlacesForBikes 2020 Community Survey**](https://www.surveygizmo.com/s3/5649932/pfbcommsvy20) today and you'll be entered to win great prizes from **[Felt Bicycles](https://feltbicycles.com/products/broam-60-2020)**, **[BikeFlights.com](https://www.bikeflights.com/)**, [**Burley**](https://www.burley.com/product/travoy/?gclid=EAIaIQobChMIodDbqqev6gIVT_DACh00iQ6aEAAYASAAEgKoIfD_BwE), [**Sena**](https://www.sena.com/product/r1-evo), [**Terrano Systems**](https://www.terranosystems.com/terrano-store/), [**Trek**](http://trekbikes.com/us/en_US/equipment/bike-accessories/bike-helmets/bontrager-wavecel-helmets/c/E399/), and PeopleForBikes.
> 
> Everyone can participate regardless of where, how or why they ride. Your feedback helps determine your city or town's score in the 2021 PlacesForBikes ratings. (Want to know how your community scored last year? Check out the **[results](http://cityratings.peopleforbikes.org/)**.)
> 
> We also want to hear from your family, friends and colleagues, so please share this link far and wide. The survey closes on **October 15**.
> 
> Together we can make bicycling better!
